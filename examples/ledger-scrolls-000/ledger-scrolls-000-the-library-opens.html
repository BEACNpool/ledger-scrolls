The Library Opens

JULY 2026 · CARDANO MAINNET

Ledger Scrolls is the free, open-source reader and writer for immutable, forever media: files stored byte-for-byte inside the Cardano blockchain, rebuilt and hash-verified by anyone, deletable by no one. As of this release, the system is fully self-hosting — and this announcement is published the only way that would be honest: as a scroll.
